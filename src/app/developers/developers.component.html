What is the Legends SDK?
Legends is the All In One VR Development Platform



Legends by SideQuest is a content distribution platform. People can download and use Legends to access content we and our members make. It has everything you need to create loyalty building multi-user cross-play content.

Legends is a new way to build your Unity Game. Legends provides a simple unified SDK for building VR games, content and experiences for standalone and desktop VR headsets. What makes SQ Legends different is how developer friendly our system is. It has been designed from the ground up to integrate with the latest Unity3D workflows and it supports C-Sharp scripting in Unity3D.


SDK Benefits
Legends is the All In One VR Development Platform

Working with the Legends SDK is great. Your project is lightweight and only contains scripts and asset you need, no additional third party scripts are required for you to have full support for all major VR headsets and a host of other features. Here are some of the benefits of using the Legends SDK over deploying a traditional standalone Unity3D game.

doneCross platform support - We already support all major desktop and mobile headsets. done3D Spatial Voice & Networking - We provide VOIP & networking pipelines so you can focus on the game logic, content and all the fun stuff! Added benefit: no server costs! doneSingle Input System - We translate inputs from a range of devices into a single set of inputs for you to use, no more multiple input systems! doneInteraction system - You can leverage the SideQuest Interaction System to allow people to spawn, grab, resize and throw objects with integrated network sync and fine controls over physics - make custom interactable objects! doneSingle click export of existing Unity projects - You can get our SDK on the Unity Asset Store and export any GameObject in your Unity3D scene to SideQuest. You can use custom scripts and MonoBehaviours to access the whole scene hierarchy at runtime! doneMonetization ( coming soon )! - Its important to get paid for your work, it helps to keep new updates coming and drives more polished content. Our SDK will allow you to monetize your content on our platform so you can keep the lights on!
